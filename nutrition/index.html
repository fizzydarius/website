My Macros

Calories: 2580 kcal. Carbs: 178g. Fats: 102g. Protein: 218g.

Chicken Wrap

The easiest, sexiset thing I eat on a day-to-day basis. One wrap, chicken lettuce, light mayo and your choice of cheese. Grill up the wrap and enjoy. Macros for this meal may vary on your serving size and how much chicken you use.

Chicken and Rice

The classic, the original, the piece the le resitance. Sautee onions, carrots, garlic, red peppers and spices to your likings until nicely cooked. Leave your white rice into warmish water for 20 minutes before cooking. Boil rice 1:2 ratio (rice:water). Season chicken, grill and cut in little cubes. Make a butter-based sauce and mix everything together when everything is done and enjoy if you want a more detailed receipe, hit me up.
