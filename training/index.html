Training

Information about my workout

There's nothing really special about my workout, I used to go to the gym and do a bit of everything and then hit legs and chest when I felt like they're not sore from the last session. Bad idea, bad thing bad darius, bad bad bad. Now I work through a PPL split. Which stands for Push Pull Legs split. I am also thinking about doing a upper body, lower body, conditioning, PPL. I aim to go gym 6 times a week, hitting each P P and L twice a week at least. I work through a Push A - Push B , Pull A - Pull B, Legs A - Legs B. I update, edit and change my workout every week, switching up exercises I don't feel comfortable doing or if I find another exercise that I feel is better than my current one. That's something you should also try and do.
